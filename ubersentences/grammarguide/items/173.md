## Summary

<table><tr>   <td>Summary</td>   <td>A subordinate conjunction which marks a condition that brings about an uncontrollable event or state.</td></tr><tr>   <td>Equivalent</td>   <td>If; when</td></tr><tr>   <td>Part of speech</td>   <td>Conjunction</td></tr><tr>   <td>Related expression</td>   <td>たら (ば, なら): 時</td></tr></table>
