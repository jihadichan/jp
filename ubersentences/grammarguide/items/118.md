# も (2)

## Summary

<table><tr>   <td>Summary</td>   <td>A marker which indicates emphasis.</td></tr><tr>   <td>English</td>   <td>Even; as many/much/long/...as; (not) even (one); (not) any</td></tr><tr>   <td>Part of speech</td>   <td>Particle</td></tr></table>
