## Summary

<table><tr>   <td>Summary</td>   <td>A particle that presents two possible choices or the minimum and maximum possible numbers/lengths/weights/etc. of something; or a conjunction that links two possible actions or events.</td></tr><tr>   <td>Equivalent</td>   <td>From ~ to ~; between ~ and ~; or</td></tr><tr>   <td>Part of speech</td>   <td>Particle/Conjunction</td></tr><tr>   <td>Related expression</td>   <td>から; か; または; もしくは; さもなくば; あるいは</td></tr></table>
