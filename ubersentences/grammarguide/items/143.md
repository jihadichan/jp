# する (4)

## Summary

<table><tr>   <td>Summary</td>   <td>A verb that indicates how much something costs or a duration of time.</td></tr><tr>   <td>English</td>   <td>Cost; lapse</td></tr><tr>   <td>Part of speech</td>   <td>Verb (Irregular)</td></tr></table>
