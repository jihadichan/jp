# の無さ・のなさ

## Summary

<table><tr>   <td>Summary</td>   <td>A phrase that indicates a state where something is lacking.</td></tr><tr>   <td>English</td>   <td>Lack of; absence of</td></tr><tr>   <td>Part of speech</td>   <td>Phrase</td></tr></table>
