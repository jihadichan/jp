# でも

## Summary

<table><tr>   <td>Summary</td>   <td>The て form of です plus も ‘even’.</td></tr><tr>   <td>English</td>   <td>Even</td></tr><tr>   <td>Part of speech</td>   <td>Particle</td></tr></table>
