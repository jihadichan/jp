# だって (1)

## Summary

<table><tr>   <td>Summary</td>   <td>A conjunction that indicates a reason.</td></tr><tr>   <td>English</td>   <td>Because</td></tr><tr>   <td>Part of speech</td>   <td>Conjunction (usually used in conversation)</td></tr></table>
