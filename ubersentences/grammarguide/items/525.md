## Summary

<table><tr>   <td>Summary</td>   <td>A compound particle that means “regardless of”</td></tr><tr>   <td>Equivalent</td>   <td>Regardless of; independent of; without reference to; without distinguishing; whether X or Y; whether X or not; whatever/whoever/etc.</td></tr><tr>   <td>Part of speech</td>   <td>Compound Particle (usually used in formal writing and formal speech)</td></tr><tr>   <td>Related expression</td>   <td>を問わず</td></tr></table>
